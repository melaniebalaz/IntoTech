ABOUT IntoTech

Made by geeks, for geeks.

You have always been curious about technology? You just got started with studying IT and are a bit lost? Or maybe you have a great idea for an IT project, but no idea where to start?
Join us and get inspired, meet the IT community and get into technology at our one day event in Vienna.




What's IntoTech?

IntoTech is a "get-together" kind of conference, offering enough spaces for meeting the community, exchanging ideas and getting inspired. We'll be offering a variety of talks and workshops, targeted especially at IT beginners and newbies. Check back soon for our event tracklist and registration!


Who are we?

We are a small team of tech-enthusiasts that want to create something nice for our fellow geeks. Most of us are students in various stages of an IT degree and we have all been there - at that starting point where a terminal is a magic window and code just gibberish.


How is it organized?

IntoTech is free for everyone - no admission fee, no hidden costs. All we ask from you is that you register in time. How we make all this possible? We're currently in the process of applying for a bunch of findings, and will approach sponsors in due time.


OUR VISION

We live in a world where almost everything is in some way controlled or calculcated by a computer, where most people use social media and information is shared online. And while this will increase even more in the future, a lot of people perceive IT as magic, or too difficult to do themselves. We would like to close that breach between the "users" and the "techgeeks", and inspire people to get started with IT and utilize it for their own projects, to hang in there through the maybe tough beginning and find a community to help them get further.

Afterall Tech is not that hard and it is a lot of fun to play around with, especially when you are not on your own.
