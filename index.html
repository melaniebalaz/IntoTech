ABOUT IntoTech

Made by geeks, for geeks.

You have always been curious about technology? You just got started with studying IT and are a bit lost? Come and get inspired, meet the IT community and get into technology at our 2 day event in Vienna. Short info text about the format of the Conference, how it will look.




Why IntoTech?

Short text about why we organize this, should sound magical


Who are we?

We are a small team of tech-enthusiasts that want to create something nice for our fellow geeks. Most of us are students, in various stages of an IT degree and we have all been there, at that starting point where a terminal is a magic window and code just gibberish.


How is it organized?

IntoTech is free for you to participate and so this is possible, we are in the progress of/have applied for a bunch of fundings. We are also looking for and will approach sponsors!


OUR VISION

Text about whhy we organize this (breach between user and coder, tech is hard attitude), make it easier for people to get into the community and utilize technology for their projects.


CALL FOR SPEAKERS!

Do you have a cool project that uses technology to create an impact, you would like to share with the world? Are you up to sharing some of your tech-enthusiasm by holding a workshop for IT-beginners? Are you a good public speaker and have on insight on how to teach technology, how to get people into programming or want to tell people what technology is all about?

If you share our vision, apply for a talk or workshop at IntoTech here.

Registration will open in Spring/Summer 2017. We are still in the process of setting a date and a location. You can follow us on facebook or twitter where we will release any news.
